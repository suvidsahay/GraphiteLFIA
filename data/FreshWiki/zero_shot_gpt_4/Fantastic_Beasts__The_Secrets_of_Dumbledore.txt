# Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore

## Overview

"Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore" is the third installment in the Fantastic Beasts series, a prequel to the popular Harry Potter franchise. Directed by David Yates and written by J.K. Rowling and Steve Kloves, the film follows the adventures of Newt Scamander as he battles against the dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald. ### Plot

The movie is set several years after the events of "Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald". With Grindelwald having escaped from custody and growing his followers, Albus Dumbledore enlists his former student Newt Scamander to lead a team of wizards to stop Grindelwald's plans of raising pure-blood wizards to rule over all non-magical beings.

### Cast

The movie's star-studded cast includes Eddie Redmayne as Newt Scamander, Jude Law as Albus Dumbledore, and Mads Mikkelsen as Gellert Grindelwald. Other returning actors include Katherine Waterston, Dan Fogler, and Alison Sudol.

### Production

#### Pre-production

The development of "Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore" started immediately after the release of its predecessor. J.K. Rowling and Steve Kloves collaborated to write the script while the film's casting process was underway.

#### Filming

Principal photography for the film began in September 2020 and concluded in February 2021. Filming locations included various sites in the UK, notably the Warner Bros. Studios in Leavesden.

#### Post-production

Post-production involved rigorous editing, visual effects, and sound design. The film's post-production team included top industry professionals who worked tirelessly to create a visually stunning wizarding world.

## Plot

"Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore" starts with Grindelwald, having escaped from custody, now growing his followers. He preaches about pure-blood wizards' supremacy over non-magical beings, causing a divide within the wizarding community. Dumbledore, aware of Grindelwald's threat, forms a team led by Newt Scamander to thwart Grindelwald's plans. The film is filled with magic, adventure, and suspense as the team navigates through dangerous situations.

## Cast and characters

### Main

Eddie Redmayne stars as Newt Scamander, a Magizoologist who is central to the plot. Jude Law plays Albus Dumbledore, the future Hogwarts Headmaster, while Mads Mikkelsen steps into the role of the antagonist, Gellert Grindelwald.

### Supporting

Katherine Waterston, Dan Fogler, and Alison Sudol return as Tina Goldstein, Jacob Kowalski, and Queenie Goldstein, respectively. Ezra Miller reprises his role as Credence Barebone.
